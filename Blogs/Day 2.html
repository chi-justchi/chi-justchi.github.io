Day 2: Daddy

7 April 2023

“Wow, you must be so rich” — my friend said when she saw me taking out my MacBook.

I denied immediately, but then said “Well, I behaved well so my “daddy” bought it for me”, thinking it would be a nice joke.

“Oh it’s your daddy” - my friend said with understanding.

I was mind-blowed. Hell no. Why on Earth would she really think I have a sugar daddy?

“Eh? So it’s not your daddy who bought it for you?” - she asked

Oh. I forgot. I forgot that it is normal that everyone has their own, physical, biological loving, caring dads, it is just me who dont have a “real” dad for so long that on my mind, the word “daddy” is a default for “sugar daddy”.

I guess perceptions of language can be shaped by our own experiences and biases, and I gotta be more mindful then.
